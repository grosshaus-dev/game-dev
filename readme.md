# Our Game Development Guides

*These guides exist to provide ourselves with the structure needed for productivity.*

They are meant to keep us on the same page; simply stating what should be done. They are specific to the workflow and tools we use and don't go into the conceptual *why* behind our choices, nor do they teach the practical *how*.

**Note**: We are currently still in the process of defining our development process. Critical information may still be missing or incomplete.
